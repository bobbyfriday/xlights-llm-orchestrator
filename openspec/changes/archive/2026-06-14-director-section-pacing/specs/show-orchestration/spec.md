## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: The director is steered to keep sections short
The creative-direction prompt SHALL inform the director that the song is already split into musical sections, that a look held longer than ~35s reads as static/boring to a viewer, and that it should keep sections short (≈10–35s) by default — merging adjacent sections into a longer one only as a justified exception when the music genuinely stays unified. This is guidance, not a hard cap.

#### Scenario: Director prompt carries the pacing guidance
- **WHEN** the Director composes the show plan
- **THEN** its prompt states to prefer the provided fine sections, that >~35s reads as boring, and that long sections are a justified exception
